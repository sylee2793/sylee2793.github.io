PhD Research Experience

Research Focus I

Retraction Fibers in GBM

Identified that retraction fibers (RFs) in glioblastoma cells are generated through fibronectin–integrin α5β1 interaction, with focal adhesion involvement demonstrated via live-cell FRET imaging. Showed that RFs promote the motility of neighboring GBM cells using a microfluidic device.

GBM Live-cell Imaging FRET
Research Focus II

Migrasomes & ER Stress in GBM

Discovered that migrasomal autophagosomes act as stress-relief organelles in GBM cells, distinct from classical extracellular vesicles. Demonstrated their delivery to microglial cells and their role in relieving endoplasmic reticulum stress — published in BMC Biology (2024), featured by BMC Biology for World Cancer Day 2026.
